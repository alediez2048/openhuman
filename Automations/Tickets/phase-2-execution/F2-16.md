## What This Ticket Must Accomplish

### Goal

- **Hero E2E** at `app/test/e2e/specs/workflows-phase-2-hero.spec.ts`:
    1. Fresh workspace.
    2. Open `/chat`, say "every time someone POSTs to my webhook URL, summarise the JSON and post to Slack."
    3. The drafter emits a `webhook → agent_prompt → channel_message` chain.
    4. `<WorkflowProposalPreview>` renders.
    5. Click [Save & Enable].
    6. The chain's webhook URL is exposed in the preview's `setup_instructions`.
    7. The spec POSTs to the webhook URL (using a valid HMAC).
    8. Polls `workflows_get_run` until terminal.
    9. Asserts: 3 step rows persist; final step's output contains the substituted body.

- **Catalog E2E** at `app/test/e2e/specs/workflows-phase-2-catalog.spec.ts`:
    1. Fresh workspace.
    2. `/workflows` shows 9 starter cards (4 Phase 1 + 5 Phase 2).
    3. Click [Add] on RU-7 (GitHub webhook summary).
    4. Workflow row appears; matching catalog card disappears.
    5. Direct-RPC delete; catalog row reappears.

- **Phase 2 Complete** section in `Automations/Tickets/phase-2-execution/DEVLOG.md` summarising all 16 tickets + their commit SHAs.

- **ADR drift audit** — walk every ADR + note conformance / drift. Document Phase 2-specific deviations (e.g. `condition` node's text-only ops vs an expression engine).
